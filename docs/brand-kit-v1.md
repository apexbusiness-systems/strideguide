### Product Names & Taglines
- **Product**: StrideGuide
- **Tagline (EN)**: Your seeing‑eye assistant in your pocket

- **Tagline (FR)**: Votre assistant de guidage visuel, dans votre poche

## Color System (WCAG 2.2 AA+ Compliant)

### Primary Palette
- **Primary**: #3730A3 (Indigo 800) - Logo, primary buttons, links
- **Primary Dark**: #1E1B4B (Indigo 950) - Headers, dark backgrounds
- **Accent**: #F97316 (Safety Orange) - Focus rings, key CTAs
- **Success**: #16A34A (Green 600) - Confirmations, positive states
- **Warning**: #F59E0B (Amber 500) - Alerts, caution states  
- **Critical**: #DC2626 (Red 600) - Errors, emergency states

### Surface Colors
- **Surface Light**: #FFFFFF - App backgrounds (light mode)
- **Surface Dark**: #0B0F14 - App backgrounds (dark mode)
- **Text Primary**: #0B0F14 - Text on light surfaces
- **Text Inverse**: #F8FAFC - Text on dark surfaces

### Contrast Guidelines
- Body text: Text Primary on Surface Light ≥15:1 ratio
- Buttons: White on Primary ≥4.5:1, Black on Accent ≥7:1
- Never use red/green-only indicators; always pair with icons/shapes

## Typography

### Font Stack
- **Primary UI**: System UI (SF Pro on iOS, Roboto on Android)
- **Web/Desktop**: Inter (variable weight) where available
- **Fallback**: system-ui, -apple-system, Segoe UI, Roboto, Helvetica, Arial, sans-serif

### Size Scale (Mobile-Optimized)
- **H1**: 28–32pt (seniors-friendly default)
- **H2**: 24pt
- **H3**: 20pt  
- **Body**: 18pt (senior default, WCAG AA+ for readability)
- **Caption**: 16pt

### Accessibility
- Touch targets: ≥52dp/pt minimum
- Line height: 1.5x for readability
- Letter spacing: +1% for body text

## Logo & Wordmark

### Logo Components
- **Monogram**: Stylized "SG" forming forward path arrow
- **Wordmark**: "StrideGuide" in Inter 800 weight
- **Lockups**: Monogram + Wordmark, Wordmark only, Monogram only

### Usage Rules
- Clear space: ≥height of the "S" in monogram
- Minimum sizes: 24px monogram, 96px wordmark
- No skewing, recoloring outside palette, or added shadows
- Maintain 16px safe area around all marks

### Logo Variations
```
Primary Wordmark: Inter 800, #3730A3 on white
Monogram: Gradient #3730A3 to #1E1B4B with #F97316 accent
```

## Paid Plan Branding

### Premium Badge
- **Color**: Primary (#3730A3) background with white text
- **Style**: Rounded corners, small crown or star icon
- **Placement**: Top-right of paid features, settings headers
- **Copy**: "Premium" or "PRO" (keep short)

### Upgrade CTA Styling
- **Button**: Accent (#F97316) background, high contrast text
- **Icon**: Upward arrow or unlock symbol
- **Animation**: Subtle pulse or glow effect permitted
- **Copy**: "Upgrade to Premium" or "Unlock All Features"

## Strap Incentive Assets

### Strap Product Guidelines
- **Material**: Soft neoprene or polyester, skin-safe certification
- **Colors**: Primary (#3730A3) or neutral (black/grey) with brand accent
- **Hardware**: Breakaway clasp rated 15 lbs, swivel phone attachment
- **Dimensions**: Adjustable 28-52 inches, 3/4" width standard
- **Branding**: Small embossed or printed "StrideGuide" on clasp tab

### Marketing Copy Templates

#### English
- **Headline**: "Free neck strap with Premium!"
- **Features**: "Breakaway safety clasp • Works with cases • Adjustable length"
- **CTA**: "Upgrade & get your free strap"

#### French  
- **Headline**: "Sangle de cou gratuite avec Premium!"
- **Features**: "Fermoir de sécurité • Compatible étuis • Longueur ajustable"
- **CTA**: "Passez à la version supérieure et obtenez votre sangle gratuite"

### Strap Imagery Style
- **Photography**: Clean, minimal background (white/light grey)

- **Lighting**: Soft, even lighting, no harsh shadows

## Voice & Tone

### Communication Style
- **Clear**: No jargon, direct instructions
- **Concise**: Short phrases for TTS delivery  
- **Directive**: Actionable guidance ("Veer left", "Step up")
- **Respectful**: Age-appropriate, dignity-preserving language

### Audio Cue Examples
- **English**: "Veer left", "Step up", "Stop — obstacle ahead"
- **French**: "Déviez à gauche", "Montez une marche", "Arrêt — obstacle devant"

## Application Guidelines

### Do's
- ✅ Use semantic color tokens consistently
- ✅ Maintain ≥52dp/pt touch targets
- ✅ Apply breakaway clause in all strap messaging
- ✅ Keep Premium badge subtle but visible
- ✅ Use Accent color sparingly for key actions only

### Don'ts  
- ❌ Never modify logo proportions or colors
- ❌ Don't use red/green as only differentiator
- ❌ Avoid technical jargon in user-facing copy
- ❌ Don't promise features not yet available
- ❌ Never compromise safety messaging for aesthetics

## Compliance & Legal

### Strap Safety Requirements
- Must meet breakaway force standards (12-18 lbs)
- Include safety warnings in packaging
- CPSIA compliance for children's safety (if applicable)
- Material safety certifications for skin contact

### Accessibility Compliance
- WCAG 2.2 AA minimum, AAA preferred for text
- VoiceOver/TalkBack compatibility required
- Color contrast ratios verified across all brand elements
- Focus indicators meet or exceed platform guidelines
